## Morning prep for a business trip

I did a short thinking session in the morning before a day trip.

The purpose was to organize tasks for an on-site visit at a remote office.
A vendor is doing the technical work, so my role is to attend and confirm.

I checked the schedule, related emails, and a call from the site manager.
