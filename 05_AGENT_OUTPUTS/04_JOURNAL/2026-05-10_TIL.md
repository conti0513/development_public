## 3. Framework: State Management (辻モデル)

From Prof. Tsuji (Keio) via PIVOT:

> "People don't break from events. They break from their state."

| Word | Meaning | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| Emotion | A quick signal | Just observe it |
| State | The base of brain and body | Manage it |

**Steps:**
1. Notice the emotion — it is just a signal
2. Name the state — what is really happening?
3. Take a recovery action
4. Then decide and move

**Old way:** Decide → Try hard
**New way:** Observe → Name → Recover → Decide → Small step

**Rule:** Do not make big decisions when tired.
