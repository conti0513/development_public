#### 21. Common Threat Vectors
- **Types**:
  - Email-based (phishing)
  - Network-based (malware over Wi-Fi, man-in-the-middle)
  - Supply chain (hardware/software vendor tampering)

#### 22. Phishing
- Pretending to be a trusted source via email
- **Tricks**:
  - Sense of urgency
  - Fake links and login pages
- **Defense**:
  - Check email headers
  - Donâ€™t click unknown links

#### 23. Impersonation
- Pretending to be someone (boss, support, government)
- May happen via phone or in person
- **Tip**: *"Never volunteer information without verification"*

#### 24. Watering Hole Attack
- Infects a website frequently visited by a target group
- Victim visits a **legit but compromised** site
- Common in **industry-specific** attacks

#### 25. Other Social Engineering Attacks
- **Pretexting**: Fabricated scenario to steal data
- **Misinformation/Fake News**: Social manipulation
- **Vishing (Voice Phishing)**: Phone-based scam
- **Smishing (SMS Phishing)**: SMS fraud messages
